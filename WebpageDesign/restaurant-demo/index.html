About Luna Bistro

A neighborhood cafe built around comfort and quality

We started Luna Bistro to create a relaxed dining space where good food feels approachable. From weekday lunch sets to weekend brunch sessions, every dish is prepared fresh with balanced local and international flavors.

Located in Bangsar South, we welcome families, young professionals, and food lovers looking for a place that feels warm, stylish, and easy to return to.
